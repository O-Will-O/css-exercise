About Stan Lee

Stanley Martin Lieber started his comic book career as an editorial assistant for Timely Comics at the age of 16. Soon after he was promoted to editor. By the time he had been promoted he had been writing comic book scripts for Timely under the pseudonym Stan Lee, which later became his legal name. Atlas (once timely) began to strugle financially, and in 1961, Stan Lee and Jack Kirby created The Fantastic Four. This time period is when Atlas, formerly known as Timely, would become known as Marvel, and Marvel would become a powerhouse in the comics world.

Once Marvel was created, they began a new style of collaboration known as "The Marvel Method." This Marvel Method allowed rapid releases of incredible comics such as Spider-Man, The Incredible Hulk, and The X-Men. Lee had a unique take on writing characters that would alter comicbook writing for the coming decades. Through the use of real world issues and human struggls, Stan Lee made his marvelous superhumans feel less super and more human.

Stan Lee in 2001 started moving on from just comics and woud make POW! Entertainer and would publish his autobiography Excelsior! The Amazing Life of STan Lee.Later on he would receive a Medal of Arts honor by President Georoge W. Bush, and further more would create the TV show Stan lee's Superhumans. After Disney bought Marvel and would go on to create the Marvel Cinematic Universe, Stan Lee would stay relevant to his creations by appearing in cameos across this Cinematic Universe.

Though the marvelous titan of childhoods brought joy and imagination everywhere, there was a dark side to his story. Stan Lee would lose his wife of 70 years in 2017, and soon after checked into a hospital for an irregular heartbeat and shortness of breath. Though he left the hospital and was ready to keep up with the latest Marvel movies, he had bigger, legal problems. Trusted associates and Stan lee's own daughter fought over the custody of Stan Lee's estate, pitting Lee against them all. During this time period it also came out that lee's own daughter had physically assaulted both Lee and his wife.

Stan Lee was born December 28, 1922, and died November 12, 2018. Though Stan lee himself is gone, what he did will live on forever, with his numerous stories filling the minds of people everywhere.
